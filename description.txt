Mod is a simple mod, that gives off a chat message, pop-up message and a sound effect when a Rare enemy spawns. Works on multiplayer. Made after suggestion on my Rare Drop Notification Mod by Poodik.

Currently only sound effect is vanilla Terraria Bell sound effect, more will be added with suggestions.

Open to all suggestions for improvement of the mod; What should be included in config, what other sound effects can be added, what more can be added etc.
